Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-25

This privacy notice describes how the BAETHAK marketing site ("Site") at baethak.com handles information when you visit the Site or interact with linked third-party services (for example, Google Forms). The Site is a static, client-side site used to promote the BAETHAK product and collect waitlist submissions via third-party forms.

Information we automatically receive

When you visit the Site we and the third-party providers we use may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited, referrer, and timestamps
  • Usage data collected by performance/analytics scripts

Information you provide directly

The Site itself does not provide a server-side form. When you click "Join the Waitlist" you are taken to a Google Form (or other third-party form) where you may provide personal information (for example, name and email). That information is collected and stored by the third party (Google) under their terms and policies, not by this repository directly.

Third-party services used

The Site loads resources and may integrate with the following third-party services which have their own privacy practices:

  • Google Fonts — serves fonts to the browser
  • Cloudflare CDN / cdnjs — serves GSAP scripts
  • Vercel Speed Insights — performance script (when deployed on Vercel)
  • Google Forms — waitlist submissions and any data you submit there

Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for details on their data practices.

Cookies and local storage

The Site does not set persistent cookies for authentication. Client-side scripts may use local timers/state for UI effects only. If you follow links to third-party services, those services may set cookies according to their own policies.

How we use collected information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate, secure, and optimize the Site
  • Evaluate interest in the product and manage the waitlist via third-party forms
  • Perform analytics and improve user experience (through third-party tools)

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Data you submit to third-party forms is shared with and stored by the form provider. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Data retention

We retain only non-identifying usage data needed for site operation and analytics. Personal data submitted via third-party forms is retained according to the third party's retention rules.

Your choices

You can control certain privacy choices in your browser (for example, blocking third-party cookies or disabling storage). If you want your information removed from our records, contact us using the contact method below — note that personal data held by third-party form providers must be removed through those providers.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request changes, email: privacy@baethak.com

This privacy policy applies only to the Site and not to third-party services linked from the Site. We may update this policy occasionally; the "Effective date" at the top will indicate when a change was made.
